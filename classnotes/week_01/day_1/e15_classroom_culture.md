Cohort E15 Classroom Culture

1. Phones (and laptops) on silent

2. Show up on time (If you're late, bring snacks)

3. Be polite and respectful

4. Collaborate & try to help others

5. There is no such thing as a stupid question

6. Keep your space and yourself clean

7. Don't hide your problems

8. Don't chat during classes (Open / Closed door)

9. Rome wasn't built in a day

10. Don't punch the keyboard! (Stay calm and methodical)

11. No uncovered drinks
